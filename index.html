What Ace kids parties is about?

Ace Kids Parties is a unique party performance service. We deliver fantastic birthday experiences and bring some of the greatest heroes from comics and movies to life. We provide affordable entertainment that will make your child’s birthday an adventure to remember.

Our heroes are available for events in Melbourne. Click on Price to find out more! A birthday party is all about the experience you provide. Your child will love the different presents you give them, but what will set this birthday apart from the rest? How will you make them feel? What will make them feel that way? There’s nothing like feeling special, loved, having all the presents you wanted and with your best mates around. But what if you got a present you’d only dreamed of? An experience that brings childhood fantasy to life?

Born in Favela da Rocinha, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil and have lived in Melbourne since 2011. I’ve loved superheroes for longer than I can remember. My favorite heroes were from Star Wars movies, the first time that I saw them was on a black and white Philco T.V and I started handcrafting lightsabers with recycled materials from our junk and wearing a bedsheet like a cloak.The courage it required to face danger for the sake of others, the powerful abilities that allowed them to reach beyond human potential, and the transcendence of their own limitations for the greater good. It looked really cool and it was really fun but there was a deeper reason for me watching. As the oldest child I had to stand up for my brother and sister, my mom worked full time supporting us as a single mother. No fancy parties, no fancy toys, we had to use our imagination to create our own world and adventures far away from the poor reality and violence of our hood.

I can see a superhero when a parent makes everything happen to support their family or even help another family. As a superhero myself, I represent those true heroes who when they call on me for a party, can trust me to bring light and laughter to their kids special day!

Their birthday...

...Maka.
